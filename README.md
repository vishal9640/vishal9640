  ## 🧠 About Me

  I'm **Sri Vishal Kotari**, an **AI & ML Engineer** with **3+ years of experience** designing and deploying intelligent systems end-to-end. My work spans building production-grade **LLM applications**, **RAG pipelines**, **agentic AI systems**, and **ML models** — bridging the gap between cutting-edge AI research and real-world engineering.

  I work at the intersection of **Large Language Models**, **MLOps**, and **scalable backend systems**, crafting solutions that are not just accurate but reliable, efficient, and production-ready.

  - 🤖 Building LLM agents and multi-step RAG pipelines with **LangGraph & LangChain**
  - - 🧬 Fine-tuning models using **LoRA & QLoRA** on domain-specific data
    - - ☁️ Deploying and scaling ML workloads on **AWS** with full **MLOps** pipelines
      - - 📊 Solving real-world problems with anomaly detection, time-series analysis, and streaming data
